Welcome to Open Banking

Link your account to manage all your accounts at one place. If you click on proceed, you will be redirected to OTP based login page from FinVU. We will search for your accounts based on your phone number. You can select your accounts that you want to track and manage and give consent to share data. If you are unable to find your accounts, use the option of searching accounts based on another phone number. Once consent is provided, you will be redirected back to our dashboard.
